What is Chatbot?

Backbone to develop Chatbots:
Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language process (NLP)
Gathere & Examine information:
Big data analytics

Chatbot is a popular form of AI application used by a variety of businesses for resolving issues involving conversations between the company and its clients, employees, or business partners. It can be trained to field questions from users in multiple languages. Chatbot can also interact with users via voice and images in addition to text.

Some Industries uses Chatbots

  • Real Estate: Round the clock availability, Inquiry in real-time, Better Time Management
  • Travel: Search opportunities, Book & Complete Reservation, Manage inquiries
  • Healthcare: Diagnostic Chatbots, Virtual Assistants for Healthcare Employees
  • Financial Services: Travel Planning, Financial Advisory
  • Education: Answers to students' questions quickly and efficiently
  • Restaurants: Make a restaurant reservation, place a carry out or delivery order
  • News: Exclusive news update, Upcoming events and operation information
  • Sports: Improve Communication, Provide Fast Event Information
